Live from Iceland

ShowMe Iceland aggregates live webcam feeds from Vegagerðin, LiveFromIceland, and other Icelandic sources — giving you the most complete real-time view of the island anywhere online.

ᚢ
Real-time Weather

Weather data is sourced directly from the Icelandic Meteorological Office (Veðurstofa Íslands) via their open API — the most accurate and up-to-date weather information available for Iceland.

ᚦ
Nature is Our Heritage

Iceland's dramatic landscapes — glaciers, volcanoes, geysers and northern lights — are a global heritage. ShowMe Iceland exists to share this beauty with the world, and to inspire its protection.
